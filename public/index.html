Our Philosophy

A practice built on specialization, not generalization.

Most commercial real estate transactions are run by a single broker juggling every function. We work differently. At Impact Commercial Group, each transaction is supported by a team of specialists — each accountable for a distinct discipline — working in tandem on the client's behalf.

The result is a deeper standard of care: more rigorous valuations, sharper marketing, more prepared negotiations, and better-informed counsel from start to close. It is the way we believe commercial real estate should be practiced.

Five disciplines, one team.

Every engagement draws on the same five core competencies. Each is owned by a dedicated member of our Leadership Team — so nothing is improvised, and nothing is overlooked.

i.

Valuation

Rigorous, defensible pricing grounded in comparable sales, replacement cost, and income analysis.

ii.

Marketing

Original materials, deliberate exposure, and a marketing process that reflects the asset's quality.

iii.

Negotiation

Disciplined advocacy at the table — informed by data, tempered by experience.

iv.

Research

Deep, ongoing market intelligence across Texas metros — submarkets, tenants, owners, and trends.

v.

Presentation

Clear, considered communication with clients, counterparties, and capital — at every step.
